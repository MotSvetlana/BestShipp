Our company

Best Shipp got its start in 2009, when founder Angus discovered that 50% of vans on the road were travelling half empty. He realised that filling those empty vans would create cheaper delivery costs and it would be better for the environment. Now, 8 years on, we've celebrated some amazing milestones!

Why us?

  • Easy return

    Easy return

    We help with returns if the product does not fit. If you didn't like the purchased product or it didn't fit, we will help you return it to the store. Our services are provided free of charge, the customer pays only the cost of return shipping.

  • Safe purchase and delivery

    Safe purchase and delivery

    Your order will be insured against loss and damage. We carry out a multi-level security check of each store, thus protecting our customers from unscrupulous sellers.

  • Fast delivery

    Fast delivery

    Most of the parcels delivered by us arrive on time or even earlier than the stated terms. However, in case of violation of the delivery terms, we will provide compensation in the amount of 1% of the delivery cost for each delayed working day.
